About

VbVt is a vanilla anarchy server with the goal to stay true to the vanilla survival experience.

Players can explore the world, build, make farms and invite friends to become the most established group. With no plugins to protect land and PVP constantly on, users have to protect their land, explore farther to not be found, and hide coordinates to feel protected. Without admin interference this allows users to make their own events, games and govern themselves.

Interested in Joining? Select "Add Server" and enter the following Server Address: mc.VbVt.org (1.20.1)

Server Rules
